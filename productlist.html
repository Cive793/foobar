What is Foobar about

FooBar is a modern brewpub in Copenhagen. We offer a relaxed and hygge ambiance with small outdoor sitting area that serves high quality yet affordable draught beers. The music is never too loud and the service is exceptional.

In FooBar we change the way you order the beer: instead of standing in the que you can easily order the beers on our app! You can either use the tablets at each table or download the app on your phone.

In the app you can see our current beer selection, and place your order inside FooBar. You can also choose to get a text message when your order is ready for picking-up at the bar.
